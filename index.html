1. You'll be given a number at the start of each game.
2. There are four crystals above. By clicking on crystal,
you add a specific amount of points to your total score.
3. To win, match your total score to the random number.
You'll lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of the crystals will be hidden until you click them.
Each time the game restarts, the value of the crystals will change.
